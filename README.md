# Learn to Actually Use AI

A hands-on course that takes you from your first prompt to building full-stack apps and automating your life. No jargon — just the skills that matter.

**Live at:** [savar.ai/learn](https://savar.ai/learn)

## Course Structure

### Part 1: Use It
Get from zero to your first useful result. Learn to spot when AI is wrong and fix bad output.

1. **Your First Useful Thing** — Get AI to solve a real problem in your life
2. **When AI Lies to Your Face** — How to spot confident nonsense
3. **Fix It, Don't Restart** — Diagnose bad output and make it better

### Part 2: Own It
Make AI yours. Set up memory, build reusable tools, and create a personal AI stack.

4. **Make It Remember You** — System prompts and persistent context
5. **Build Your First Tool** — Create something you'll use every week
6. Your AI Stack *(coming soon)*

### Part 3: Build With It
Use AI to build real software. Go from idea to deployed app in one conversation.

7. Claude Code: Your Coding Partner *(coming soon)*
8. From Idea to Deployed App *(coming soon)*
9. When Things Break *(coming soon)*

### Part 4: Automate Everything
Connect AI to your entire workflow. Commands, hooks, integrations, parallel agents.

10. Commands, Hooks, and Workflows *(coming soon)*
11. Connect Your Whole Life *(coming soon)*
12. Working in Parallel *(coming soon)*
